# 🌐 Company Website Plan

**Coffee & Health Supplement Business**

---

## 📌 Overview

This website is designed to:

- Generate leads (Telegram, phone, social)
- Build strong brand credibility
- Showcase imported coffee and health supplements
- Target both local customers and business clients in Cambodia

---

## 🎯 Website Concept

> **“Premium lifestyle + trusted health brand”**

The website should:

- Build trust quickly (especially for supplements)
- Create a premium and appealing coffee lifestyle image
- Encourage users to contact and order بسهولة

---

## 🧱 Website Structure

### 1. 🏠 Homepage (Most Important)

**Hero Section**

- Headline:

  > Premium Imported Coffee & Health Supplements in Cambodia

- Subtext:

  > Boost your energy, focus, and wellness with trusted global products

- CTA Buttons:
  - Shop Now
  - Contact Us

**Sections**

- ⭐ Featured Products (Coffee + Supplements)
- 💪 Key Benefits (Energy, Focus, Immunity)
- 🌍 Imported & Trusted (build credibility)
- 🧾 Testimonials
- 📞 Quick Contact CTA

---

### 2. 🛍️ Products Page

Split into categories:

#### ☕ Coffee

#### 💊 Supplements

Each product should include:

- High-quality images
- Benefits (not just description)
- Product origin (country imported from)
- Usage instructions (especially supplements)
- CTA:
  - Order via Telegram
  - Contact for bulk purchase

---

### 3. 👤 About Us

Purpose: Build trust

Include:

- Company story
- Why the business started
- Product sourcing (import details)
- Quality assurance
- Mission statement:

  > Bring high-quality international wellness products to Cambodia

---

### 4. 🌟 Testimonials / Reviews

Important for credibility, especially supplements.

Include:

- Customer quotes
- Photos (if available)
- Real experiences (avoid exaggeration)

---

### 5. 📞 Contact Page

Make it easy to reach you:

- Phone number
- Telegram / Facebook
- Google Maps location
- Simple contact form

---

### 6. 🧠 Blog / Education (Optional but Highly Recommended)

Purpose:

- Improve SEO
- Build authority
- Educate customers

Example topics:

- Benefits of black coffee
- How supplements improve energy
- Best time to take vitamins

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🌐 Multi-language Support

- Khmer 🇰🇭
- English 🇬🇧

---

### 📲 Simple Ordering System

Start simple:

- Order via Telegram
- Call Now
- Facebook Message

(No need for full e-commerce at the beginning)

---

### 🔒 Trust Signals (Critical for Supplements)

- Imported from [country]
- Certifications (if available)
- Real customer reviews

---

## 🎨 Design Style

Avoid:

- ❌ Overly corporate look
- ❌ Too plain or generic

Use:

- ✅ Clean and modern layout
- ✅ Lifestyle-focused visuals
- ✅ Warm coffee tones (brown, beige)
- ✅ Health colors (green, white)

## 🧠 Business Strategy

Focus on:

1. Build trust (content + testimonials)
2. Show lifestyle (coffee + health)
3. Make ordering easy

---

## 🔄 User Flow

1. User visits website
2. Sees premium branding and product benefits
3. Builds trust through content and reviews
4. Views product details
5. Contacts via Telegram / phone
6. You close the sale manually
